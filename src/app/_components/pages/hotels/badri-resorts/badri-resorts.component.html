Description

Badriville Resort has eco-sensitive individual cottages made of bamboo. Each cottage is spacious, offering you quiet and cosy with undisturbed view of the town, temple and overpowering Neelkanth peak. For the more adventurous ones, you can also rent trekking gear or even a mountain bike.
